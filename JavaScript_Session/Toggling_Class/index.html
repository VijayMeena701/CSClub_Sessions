I'm Naruto Uzumaki

I am a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan. I became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of my birth — a fate that caused me to be shunned by most of Konoha throughout my childhood. After joining Team Kakashi, I worked hard to gain the village's acknowledgement all the while chasing my dream to become Hokage. In the following years, through many hardships and ordeals, I became a capable ninja regarded as a hero both by the villagers, and soon after, the rest of the world, becoming known as the Hero of the Hidden Leaf. I also proved to be one of the main factors in winning the Fourth Shinobi World War, leading me to achieve his dream and become the village's Nanadaime Hokage.
